Meet The Team

Ben Jackson

@benjack94

Ben started the podcast as a solo enterprise initially before thankfully being joined by Scott and Joris. Having spent some time writing for Total Football Analysis covering the Pro League, Ben realised that there wasn’t an English podcast covering the league and decided that it was time to do something about that. Like Scott, Ben is a neutral, but has travelled to both Brugge and Brussels. His only live experience of Belgian football was seeing a friendly between KAA Gent and Sunderland back in 2002, however a half time pitch invasion by the Sunderland fans meant that 7 year-old Ben never got to see the second half!

Scott Coyne

@scott_coyne

Although based in Scotland, Scott has a deep love of both football and Belgian culture...it was perhaps inevitable those loves would meet! He has been lucky enough to travel and spend time in Belgium, including Brussels, Gent, Antwerp, Mechelen and Brugge...he also enjoys his Belgian beer, but that's a whole other story!

Joris Becq

@Joris_Becq

The Belgian flavour from the podcast, mostly by blood since he lives in Hamburg lately, after some spells in Estonia and Russia. Unfortunately not as a football player, but still always on the move, and he is a polyglot which helps him in several ways! As a Racing Genk fan, he is the least impartial of the three, although he tries his best to leave his preference off the mic. Something else he sometimes fails at before the microphones, is hiding his cynicism. Dares to randomly start shouting hot takes and is a firm believer in “the jinx”, showing his superstitiousness in the sporting field.
